Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How can I contribute content to Verdant Blog?

+

We welcome guest contributions from writers passionate about sustainability! Please email your pitch or article idea to submissions@verdantblog.com with the subject line "Guest Post Submission." Include a brief bio and any relevant writing samples.

Do you offer advertising or sponsorship opportunities?

+

Yes, we partner with eco-friendly brands and organizations whose values align with ours. For advertising inquiries, please contact partnerships@verdantblog.com with details about your company and objectives.

How frequently do you publish new content?

+

We publish new articles twice weekly, typically on Tuesdays and Fridays. Our newsletter goes out every Sunday with a roundup of the week's content and exclusive tips.

Can I request an article on a specific sustainability topic?

+

Absolutely! We value reader input and are always looking for new topics to explore. Use the contact form above to send us your article suggestions or topic requests.

Do you offer consulting services for sustainable practices?

+

Several of our team members offer consulting services for individuals and businesses looking to implement more sustainable practices. Please email consulting@verdantblog.com for more information about our rates and services.
